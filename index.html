03 My Process

Design is a conversation
between people and products.

01

Discover

User interviews, competitive analysis, heuristic evaluation. I listen before I draw. Understanding the problem space deeply is the foundation of every good solution.

  • User Interviews
  • Contextual Inquiry
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Stakeholder Workshops
02

Define

Synthesis, affinity mapping, personas, and journey maps. I turn raw research into clear design principles and prioritized opportunities that the whole team rallies around.

  • Affinity Mapping
  • User Personas
  • Journey Maps
  • Jobs-to-be-Done
03

Design

From rough sketches to high-fidelity interactive prototypes. I iterate fast, test early, and embrace feedback. The best designs are never finished on the first try.

  • Sketching & Wireframing
  • High-Fi Prototypes
  • Design Systems
  • Micro-interactions
04

Validate

Usability testing, A/B experiments, accessibility audits. Shipping is not the finish line. I measure outcomes and use data to inform the next iteration cycle.

  • Usability Testing
  • A/B Testing
  • Accessibility Audits
  • Analytics Review
